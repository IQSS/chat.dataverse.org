More Information

chat.dataverse.org is the landing page for Dataverse Chat, but the conversation happens in the #dataverse room on matrix.org, the flagship server for a federated, open standard communication protocol called Matrix.

For transparency, logs of the conversation are public and can be seen at view.matrix.org.

Your ideas about chat are welcome! Please give feedback in chat or open an issue.
